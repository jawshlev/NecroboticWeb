Necrobotic

Game Idea

Necrobotic is a metroidvania themed around the pursuit of power and the risks of losing yourself as a result. The player will be able to improve their capabilities by ‘upgrading’ themselves, but the more they do so, the faster they will lose energy, and the more they lose touch with their humanity.
