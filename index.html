Necrobotic

Game Idea

Necrobotic is a thrilling fusion of necromancy and robotics, where players control undead machines in a post-apocalyptic world. Combine the arcane arts with advanced technology to survive and thrive in this unique gaming experience.

Final Product Vision

Our final product will be a fully immersive 3D action-adventure game, featuring:

  • Unique necrobot crafting system
  • Expansive post-apocalyptic open world
  • Engaging storyline with multiple endings
  • Multiplayer co-op and PvP modes
